FinTech FAQs

What is FinTech?
FinTech refers to technology used to improve and automate financial services like payments, banking, and investments.
How secure are online transactions?
Online transactions use encryption, OTP authentication, and secure payment gateways to keep your money safe.
What is KYC and why is it required?
KYC (Know Your Customer) verifies the identity of users to prevent fraud and ensure safe financial operations.
Can I track my spending?
Yes, fintech apps allow users to monitor expenses, manage budgets, and analyze spending patterns.
Is my financial data safe?
FinTech platforms use strong security systems, data encryption, and privacy policies to protect user information.
